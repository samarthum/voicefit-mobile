02 · Design principles

4 commitments
P1
Voice first, not voice only.
Every voice action has a tap / keyboard equivalent one layer deep. Voice is the fast path, not the lock-in.
P2
Confidence, not certainty.
When Claude isn't sure, say so — flag "estimated" plainly, never hide it. Trust is earned by honesty, not polish.
P3
Under-celebrate.
Dark surfaces, one bright accent, no gamification dopamine. Real progress is quiet. PR banners earn their moment.
P4
Fewer numbers.
Show the one stat that answers "am I on track?" — bury the rest behind taps. Density is the enemy of glanceability.
